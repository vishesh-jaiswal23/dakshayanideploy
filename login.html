Need a portal account?

Self-registration is disabled to protect customer data. The operations team will provision access for trusted partners.

To request access:

  • Email connect@dakshayani.co.in with your name, organisation, and role.
  • The admin team will verify your details and send a secure activation link.
  • Once approved, you can set your password and log in from this page.

If you already received credentials, sign in using the form on the left.

Secure access checklist

  1. Confirm that the portal server has the correct administrator email and password set in its .env configuration.
  2. Enter the same email address and password shared with you by the operations team.
  3. Click Log in. A success message will appear and you will be redirected to the administrator dashboard.
  4. If you see an error, contact connect@dakshayani.co.in for support.
