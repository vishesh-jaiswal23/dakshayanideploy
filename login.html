How to log in

  1. Pick the matching role from the Choose a portal dropdown (Admin, Customer, Employee, Installer, or Referral partner).
  2. Enter the email address and password for that role. You can tap one of the Quick demo tiles or the Fill admin demo button to auto-fill credentials instantly.
  3. Hit Log in. A success message appears and the portal redirects you to the correct dashboard.
  4. If the backend API is offline (for example on static cPanel hosting), the same form validates the demo accounts right in your browser.

How to sign up

  1. Fill in your name, email, and a strong password in the Create a new account form.
  2. Add your phone number and city (optional) so the Dakshayani team can reach you with project updates.
  3. Select the role that best fits you. Customers can track project progress, referral partners monitor rewards, installers and employees manage daily work.
  4. Submit the form to create your portal login. Successful signups immediately open the chosen dashboard. If the live API is unavailable, email connect@dakshayani.co.in and the team will provision access manually.
