The Meera GH2 Value Chain

Three essential areas where we are building expertise and offering solutions.

Green Hydrogen Production

Development and integration of Alkaline and PEM electrolyzer systems for small-to-mid-scale decentralized Green Hydrogen generation, powered by renewable sources like our solar installations.

Fuel Cell Technology & Kits

Procurement and distribution of PEM fuel cells and related components. This includes educational kits for R&D institutions to foster future talent in the region.

Hydrogen EPC & Consultancy

Offering EPC services for hydrogen storage, piping, and dispensing infrastructure. Utilizing Dakshayani's structured project management for complex, multi-fuel initiatives.

Exploring Strategic Partnerships

We are actively seeking technology collaborations and strategic investment for Meera GH2's expansion across India, leveraging government initiatives like the National Green Hydrogen Mission.
