The Scheme at a Glance

The Government of India's PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana aims to promote rooftop solar installations nationwide by providing significant financial assistance, ensuring every household can benefit from free electricity and contribute to a greener India.

Key Highlights

  • Up to ₹78,000 subsidy for 3 kW systems.
  • Access to low-interest bank loans.
  • 25 years of assured savings.
  • 300 units of free electricity per month for eligible users.
