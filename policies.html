Terms & Conditions (T&C)

Agreement for Solar EPC Services.

These terms govern the contractual relationship between Dakshayani Enterprises and the customer regarding the Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) of solar photovoltaic systems.

Scope of Work

The project scope will be explicitly defined in the Statement of Work (SOW) attached to the final agreement, including system capacity, component specifications, and installation timeline.

Payment Terms

Payment milestones are typically divided into three stages: Booking/Design (20%), Procurement/Start of Work (70%), and Commissioning/Handover (10%), unless otherwise specified in the financial agreement.

Warranty Details

Assurance on System Performance and Components.

We provide a comprehensive warranty package that includes two primary coverages:

1. Performance Warranty

A 25-year linear performance warranty is offered directly by the panel manufacturer, guaranteeing a minimum power output percentage over the period.

2. Workmanship Warranty

Dakshayani Enterprises provides a 5-year workmanship warranty covering the structural integrity of the mounting, wiring, and installation quality.
