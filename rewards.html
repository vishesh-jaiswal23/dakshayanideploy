How it works

Four guided steps take your referral from lead to payout, with transparency at every stage.

  • Submit your referral

    Log into the referrer portal and add the prospect's contact information, load profile, and preferred callback time.

  • Lead qualification

    Our sales engineers conduct a discovery call within 24 hours and schedule a site visit if the customer is ready.

  • Project onboarding

    Once the customer signs, you can view milestone updates, documents, and expected commissioning date inside your dashboard.

  • Payout & recognition

    Rewards are credited digitally within 48 hours of commissioning. Hit milestones to unlock bonuses and community spotlight features.
